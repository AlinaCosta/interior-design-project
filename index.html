About

Born from the idea of making life beautiful.

Studio McGee is a full-service interior design firm that designs homes from concept to completion all over the US. We have garnered a reputation for beautifully balanced spaces that feel refreshingly modern and endlessly classic. We create custom interiors for full new builds and renovations that stand the test of time.

Based out of Salt Lake City, our team has designed hundreds of projects across 33 states (and counting), which has led us to develop and refine a seamless process to help our clients navigate each step. Our designers collaborate from the floor plan development, interior drawings, custom cabinetry, finishes, and selections: furniture, window treatments, styling, and turn-key installation.

Services

We believe that good design tells a story.

From top-to-bottom remodels to new builds, our team specializes in creating spaces that bring form and function to the everyday. We’re passionate about making your vision a reality through design that speaks to you and your lifestyle. With close attention to detail every step of the way, our process is fueled by collaboration, merging your taste with our expertise.

Learn More chevron_right

ARCHITECTURAL REVIEW

We work with your architect to review electrical plans, angles, and space planning so that every detail functions with the end goal in mind, from flow to floor plan.

INTERIOR DESIGN

Our team takes the guesswork out of choosing all interior finishes, from hardwood to grout color. We'll work with you to sketch and select all of the nitty-gritty details.

STYLING

Our styling team comes in for the finishing touches to elevate your design and tell your story; we like to think of it as the cherry on top.

EXTERIOR DESIGN

We'll help you choose materials and their placement for your exterior to create a beautiful and cohesive facade.

FURNITURE SELECTION

We select all of the furnishings you need in your home from upholstery to dining chairs, incorporating any heirloom or sentimental pieces you have in the process.

PRODUCT ORDERING & PROJECT MANAGEMENT

We'll handle all of the details of the process from start to finish to ensure that everything is organized, on time, and on track.
